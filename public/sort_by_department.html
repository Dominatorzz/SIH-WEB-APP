Medanta Medicity

Medanta is an Indian chain of multi-specialty medical institutes based in Gurgaon. Medanta was started in 2009 with one institute, Medanta - The Medicity, in Gurgaon, with cardiac surgeon Naresh Trehan as its main director along with co-founder Sunil Sachdeva.
